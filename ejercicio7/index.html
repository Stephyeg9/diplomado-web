World Cup 2018

Thinking of visiting Rusia?

Are you planning to visit Russia for the 2018 FIFA World Cup™? Welcome2018.com is the tourist portal of the Russian government and provides fans travelling to Russia with useful information about the host nation of the next FIFA World Cup.

In the Russian capital, one may find all kinds of souvenirs, to say nothing of those that have already become a traditional must-have of the foreign tourist: earflapped fur hats, bear figurines, matryoshkas and balalaikas.
